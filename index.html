Event Guideline

Hello Folks!!
Sona's yearly cultural festival, Krivvas'23, is back and better than ever. Indeed, it is offline as well. Every year, Sona-Tech students organise Krivvas, and we made sure that everyone who attended the offline event the previous year had a great time. The wait is now over. We hope to make Krivvas'23 an outstanding experience for you by selecting the ideal blend of cultural events. You won't miss anything, so trust us when we say that. Visit our website because it contains all the answers to the questions you might have. Keep tuned and get ready for Krivvas'23 to take you on a trip of a lifetime!
